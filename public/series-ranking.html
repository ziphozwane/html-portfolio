The Best Series I've Seen in 2023

My top 3 seasons this year

The Last of Us

An accurate retelling of the original videogame, with outstanding story additions. Perfectly cast throughout.

The Fall of House of Usher

Finnegan back at his best. The cast returns for a new paranormal story. Turned up the gore this time around.

Jury Duty

The only "reality TV" show I have managed to get through. Fantastic acting throughout.

Fisk

Delightfully funny show from Australian comedian.
